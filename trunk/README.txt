This plugin is an addon to OneSignal, offres segmenting your one signal user's data by Tag for your Pages post and Customs Post.

== Description ==

This plugins will send "Category" of the post as the Tag of the users of your post to your onesignal account, only if user allow to recevie Push notification from your website. Enable Tags for post page and custom posts for your one signal user data, and send notification using tags. 

It would help you to send push notification to only those people who have visited those catagory in past due to your business need. 

Plugin let you decide if you also want to send the notification to your full audiences at the Post Level.

ONCE Master, let you enable push notification for your pages as well in the settings.

You may like:

1. Send Tag for each page and post and custom post types
2. Send push notification to only people having the respective Tags
3. Ability to send message to all of your users bypassing all filters
4. More data about what your users view on your site
6. Let you send push notificaiton for Pages 
7. You will also know what pages are viewed by your users 
8. Ability send Yost Desc as the message in Push notification
9. More to come, you can also send your request/query piclaunch@gmail.com
